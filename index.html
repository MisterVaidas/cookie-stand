About Us

Awesome Cookie Stand has been serving the most delicious cookies since 2020. Our business started out as a small family-operated cookie stand in Seattle. Inspired by our love for baking and passion for quality, we've been able to grow and now proudly operate cookie stands in five locations worldwide, including Tokyo, Dubai, Paris, and Lima.

We're committed to bringing our customers the best, using only high-quality ingredients and time-tested recipes. Each cookie is a product of our dedication, from the moment the ingredients are mixed to when the freshly baked cookies are taken out of the oven. We believe that a great cookie can make any day better, and we strive to create cookies that not only taste good, but also bring a smile to your face.

At Awesome Cookie Stand, we understand that every cookie we make is an opportunity to bring joy to our customers. We love what we do and we're excited to share our cookies with you. Come and visit us at any of our locations, or get in touch if you have any questions. We can't wait to serve you!
